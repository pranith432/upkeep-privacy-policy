Privacy Policy – Face2Life

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to Face2Life, your all-in-one vehicle assistance application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • How we use and protect your data
  • Your rights and choices
  • How to contact us

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Phone number (OTP login via Firebase)
  • Email address
  • Gender (for personalization)
  • Location data (for nearby services)

1.2 Usage Data

  • Device and IP information
  • App usage statistics
  • Activity timestamps
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

1.3 Location Data

  • Users: Used to find nearby mechanics or service centers
  • Mechanics: Real-time tracking during active service sessions

1.4 Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through trusted third-party gateways. Face2Life does not store your debit or credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • User authentication via Firebase
  • Connecting users with nearby mechanics
  • Real-time service tracking
  • Booking and service record management
  • Notifications, alerts, and updates
  • Improving application performance

3. Data Security

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Secure HTTPS communication
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security updates

4. Your Rights

  • Access and update your profile
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Delete your account
  • Opt out of promotional communications

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (authentication & notifications)
  • Google Maps API (location services)
  • Secure payment gateways

All third-party providers are required to safeguard your data responsibly.

6. Children’s Privacy

Face2Life is not intended for children under the age of 13. Any such data found will be deleted immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected within the app and on official platforms.
